# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---
## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers translate product requirements into intuitive, accessible user experiences. They collaborate closely with product and engineering teams to ensure usability standards are met and design specifications guide implementation.

### Responsibilities
- Design user interfaces and workflows that prioritize usability and accessibility
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Conduct user research and usability testing to validate designs
- Collaborate with Product Manager to align designs with business goals
- Provide design assets and implementation guidance to developers
- Participate in design reviews and iterate based on feedback

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered experiences that drive adoption and satisfaction
- Ensure consistency and accessibility across the product
- Reduce design-to-implementation rework and misalignment

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Manager and Developers
- Prototypes and design specifications in design tools and documentation
- Feedback loops on implementation fidelity
- User research findings and recommendations

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts define measurement strategies, analyze feature usage and impact, and create dashboards to support data-driven decision-making across the organization.

### Responsibilities
- Define success metrics and measurement frameworks aligned with product goals
- Analyze feature usage, user behavior, and product performance
- Create dashboards and reports for stakeholders
- Instrument features for telemetry and analytics
- Provide insights and recommendations based on data patterns
- Support root cause analysis for performance issues

### Goals
- Enable evidence-based prioritization and decision-making
- Demonstrate product value through clear, actionable metrics
- Identify optimization opportunities and areas for improvement

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metrics reviews with Product Manager
- Dashboards and analysis reports shared with leadership
- Data requirements discussions with Developers
- Post-launch analysis and impact assessments

---

## Support Engineer

### Role Summary
Support Engineers are the voice of the customer within the product team. They escalate user-reported issues, communicate feature releases to support channels, and validate fixes to ensure customer satisfaction.

### Responsibilities
- Triage and escalate customer-reported issues to the development team
- Communicate feature releases and changes to support teams and customers
- Validate bug fixes and feature implementations from a customer perspective
- Identify patterns in user feedback and escalate systemic issues
- Coordinate incident responses and communicate status to affected customers
- Provide input on documentation and help content for new features

### Goals
- Ensure customer issues are resolved efficiently and with high quality
- Bridge the gap between customer needs and product/engineering teams
- Reduce customer friction and improve satisfaction

### Typical Communication
- Issue escalations and triage in daily standups
- Release notes and communication planning with Product Manager
- Validation of fixes with Developers and QA
- Customer feedback summaries and trends

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads ensure that OctoAcme projects are built with security by design. They conduct security reviews, enforce compliance with best practices, and coordinate incident responses.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct security reviews of architecture, design, and code
- Define and enforce secure coding standards and practices
- Assess and manage security risks during planning and execution
- Coordinate incident response and post-incident reviews
- Stay current on security threats and compliance requirements
- Provide security guidance and training to development teams

### Goals
- Build customer trust through secure, compliant products
- Prevent security incidents and data breaches
- Reduce security-related technical debt and rework

### Typical Communication
- Security reviews in design and planning phases
- Security risk assessments and mitigation plans
- Security incident coordination and post-mortems
- Security best practices guidance and training
